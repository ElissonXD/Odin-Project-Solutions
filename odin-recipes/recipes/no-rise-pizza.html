No-Rise Pizza

Original source: No-Rise Pizza

Description

Quick pizza recipe without needing to wait the time for the dough to rise

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Gather all the Ingredients
  2. Combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl. Mix in warm water and oil until dough comes together.
  3. Before continuing, preheat the oven to 375 degrees C
  4. Pat and strech dough onto a large pizza pan
  5. Cover with pizza sauce and top with your desired toppings, like cheese and pepperoni
  6. Now that the oven is preheated, oven until crust is lightly browned, it takes 20 to 25 minutes

Bon appetit
